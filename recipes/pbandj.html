Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

A classic childhood sandwich recipe that just about anyone loves

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Jelly
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Get out two slices of bread
  2. Toast the bread if you prefer
  3. Use a knife to spread jelly on one slice
  4. Use a knife to spread the peanut butter on the other slice
  5. Put both slices together to complete the sandwich
