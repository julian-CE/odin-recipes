Easy Home Fries

Description

Quick and easy homefries you could have with breakfast

Ingredients

  • 3 Russet Potatoes
  • 3 Tbsps Butter
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cube the potatoes
  2. Rinse the cubed potatoes and drain water well
  3. Melt butter in skillet on medium heat
  4. Add potatoes to skillet
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste and mix well
  6. Cover and let cook for 10 minutes
  7. Remove lid and cook, turning frequently for about 10 minutes or until brown and crisp on all sides
