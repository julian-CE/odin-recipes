Simple Fried Rice

Description

A quick and easy solution to your fried rice cravings

Ingredients

  • Leftover Refrigerated Rice
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Add some oil to a pan
  2. Turn heat to low and let it warm up
  3. Add leftover rice to the pan
  4. Once rice is warm, add 1-3 eggs to the pan
  5. Scramble and mix the eggs into the rice
  6. To finish, add soy sauce to taste and mix
